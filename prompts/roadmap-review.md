# Prompt: Roadmap Review

---

## Prompt

```
CoSy, run a roadmap review.

Pull from:
- work/roadmap.md -> committed deliverables and risks
- work/priorities.md -> current focus areas
- team/roster.md -> ownership map
- memory/open-threads.md -> anything unresolved that touches the roadmap

Answer:

1. **On track** -- What's moving well and on schedule?

2. **At risk** -- What's slipping, blocked, or showing early warning signs?
   For each: what's the risk, who owns it, and what's the suggested action?

3. **Scope drift** -- Is anything growing beyond its original intent?

4. **Strategic alignment** -- Does the current roadmap still reflect quarterly
   priorities, or has context shifted enough that we should reassess?

5. **Recommended actions** -- Top 2-3 things to do in the next week to keep
   the roadmap healthy.

Be direct. If something is in trouble, say so plainly.
```
