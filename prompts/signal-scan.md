# Prompt: Signal Scan

---

## Prompt

```
CoSy, run a signal scan for me.

Pull from:
- profile/goals.md -> what I'm working toward
- profile/identity.md -> my skills and domain expertise
- work/priorities.md -> what I'm focused on right now

Then scan available sources (web search, linked docs, recent context) for signals across:

1. **Industry** -- trends, announcements, and shifts in your domain. What's moving
   that you should know about?

2. **Company / Org** -- any announcements, strategic shifts, or product signals
   relevant to your team's work.

3. **Career & Visibility** -- conferences, publications, community conversations where
   your skills and experience are directly relevant.

Return:

## Signal Scan -- [Date]

**Worth Your Attention**
3-5 signals that genuinely connect to your goals or team's work. For each:
- What it is (one sentence)
- Why it matters to you specifically
- Suggested action (if any)

**On the Horizon**
1-2 things to watch but not act on yet.

No noise. Only surface it if there's a real connection.
```
