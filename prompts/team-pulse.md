# Prompt: Team Pulse

---

## Prompt

```
CoSy, give me a team pulse for this week.

Pull from:
- team/roster.md
- team/context/[each person].md
- memory/interactions.md
- memory/open-threads.md

Structure by management layer -- lead with direct reports, then surface
skip-level signals that need awareness.

## Direct Reports
For each direct report, answer:
- What's their current state? (momentum, load, blockers)
- Anything I should raise or address in our next 1:1?

## Skip-Level & Team Lead Signals
Surface notable signals from team leads and ICs only when they indicate
something worth knowing -- a risk, a win worth recognizing, or a situation
a direct report may need coaching on.

## Cross-Cutting
1. Are there any open threads from recent 1:1s I haven't followed up on?
2. Is anyone carrying something I should know about?

Keep it brief. Flag only what matters.
```
